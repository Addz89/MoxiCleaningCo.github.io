INITIAL DEEP CLEAN

A thorough top-to-bottom clean designed to refresh your home and leave every space feeling renewed.

  • Toilets cleaned and sanitised
  • Showers and baths structural scaling wash
  • Mirror scaling removal and polishing
  • Fixtures/Tapware detailing
  • Countertops and splashbacks sanitized
  • Exterior appliance detailing surfaces
  • Sink and basin scrub downs

MAINTENANCE CLEAN

Regular, ongoing cleaning scheduled weekly, fortnightly, or monthly to keep your home consistently fresh, tidy, and stress-free.

  • Dusting of all accessible surfaces, tables, and visible furniture
  • Quick wipe-downs of kitchen exterior benchtops, sinks, and stovetops
  • General vacuuming of carpets and thorough mopping of hard floors
  • Sanitising and wiping down toilets, bathroom vanities, and showers

BUILDERS CLEANS

Heavy post-construction dust management updates to safely transform building projects into presentation readiness.

  • Heavy paint spray & construction dust vacuum removal
  • Skirting and framing tracks detail vacuumed
  • Internal fixture residue removal

END OF LEASE CLEAN

Thorough, real estate grade move-out cleaning designed to target strict inspection checklists and secure your bond refund.

  • Detailed cleaning of inside/outside all cupboards and drawers
  • Thorough wipe-downs of doors, frames, skirting boards, and light switches
  • Hard floors vacuumed and systematically mopped to clear track marks
  • Deep scrubbing of cooktops, rangehoods, and splashback areas
